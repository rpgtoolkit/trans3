About the Toolkit — Links

On this page, we have attempted to list several relevant sites, which you, as an RPGToolkit user, may find of interest.

ToolkitZone ToolkitZone, or TKZ as it is more commonly referred to, is the official home of the RPGToolkit. New versions, resources of all kinds, and a friendly, warm community await you here.
MysticSoftware MysticSoftware (also known as MS) was the original resource site of the RPGToolkit community. Today, the site still houses the largest collection of Toolkit games—all ever created, the good and the bad.
